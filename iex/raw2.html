▶ Task 2 ◀                                   Carries 20 Marks
Write about one of the following   A OR B in about 100 Words.

(A) 
Write a 100 word story about a a day in the desert:
  • When did you go to the desert and why did you choose to go there?
  • What was the weather like during your day in the desert?
  • Did you encounter any wildlife during your day in the desert? If so, what kind of animals did you see?
  • Did you have any special equipment or supplies with you to help you navigate the desert environment?
  • What kind of challenges did you face during your day in the desert, such as extreme temperatures, dehydration, or getting lost?
  • What kind of emotions did you experience during your day in the desert, such as awe, fear, or excitement?

⚠  ⚠   ____________________________ ⚠  ⚠ 

(B)

One of your friends is called Bill sent you a letter describing his favourite TV show.
Write back telling him about your favourite TV program?

  • What is your favorite TV show and why do you enjoy it?
  • When did you first start watching your favorite TV show?
  • Who is your favorite character in your favorite TV show and why?
  • What themes or messages does your favorite TV show explore?
  • How has your favorite TV show impacted your life?
  • Would you recommend your favorite TV show to others? If so, why?
  • Write about 100 words.
